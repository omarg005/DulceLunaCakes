My Journey

The Beginning

It all started in my grandmother's kitchen, where I learned the magic of combining simple ingredients to create something extraordinary. Her patience and love for baking inspired me to pursue this craft.

Formal Training

I pursued formal culinary education, specializing in pastry arts and cake decorating. This foundation gave me the skills to turn my passion into a professional craft.

Dulce Luna Cakes

In 2020, I launched Dulce Luna Cakes, combining my love for baking with my desire to create meaningful experiences for others. Every cake is a celebration of love, creativity, and joy.
