Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order my cake?

For custom cakes, we recommend ordering at least 2-3 weeks in advance. For wedding cakes or large orders, 4-6 weeks notice is preferred to ensure we can accommodate your vision.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery throughout South Florida. Delivery fees vary based on location and cake size. We also offer pickup from our kitchen for your convenience.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can create cakes that are gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and accommodate other dietary restrictions. Just let us know during your consultation.

What's your pricing structure?

Our pricing varies based on size, complexity, and ingredients. Simple cakes start around $50, while elaborate custom designs can range from $200-500+. We'll provide a detailed quote after our consultation.

Do you make wedding cakes?

Yes! Wedding cakes are one of our specialties. We create elegant, custom wedding cakes that match your theme and serve your guest count. Wedding consultations are complimentary.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our cake request form or contact us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, provide a quote, and start planning your perfect cake!
